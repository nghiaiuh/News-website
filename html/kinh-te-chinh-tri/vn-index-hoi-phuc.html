Chung khoan

VN-Index hoi phuc, dong tien phan hoa ro o nhom von hoa vua va nho

Tac gia: Hai Yen Ngay dang: 22/04/2026 6 phut doc

Thi truong chung khoan dang co nhung phien hoi phuc tich cuc sau giai doan dao dong manh, tuy nhien dong tien chua lan toa dong deu. Nhom co phieu tai chinh, ha tang va mot so doanh nghiep co ket qua kinh doanh on dinh van thu hut su quan tam ro hon mat bang chung.

Dong tien van uu tien tinh chac chan

Nha dau tu dang co xu huong giai ngan co chon loc, uu tien doanh nghiep co cau chuyen ro ve loi nhuan, don hang va kha nang mo rong bien loi nhuan. Nhom co phieu dau co hoac tang nong truoc do tiep tuc doi mat ap luc chot loi, cho thay tam ly phong thu van hien huu.

Su hoi phuc cua chi so la tin hieu tich cuc, nhung chat luong dong tien moi la yeu to quyet dinh thi truong co ben hay khong.

Nha dau tu can theo doi gi tiep theo

Bao cao ket qua kinh doanh, mat bang lai suat va dinh huong dieu hanh ty gia se la ba bien so duoc theo doi sat trong thoi gian toi. Thi truong co the duy tri trang thai phan hoa neu cac nhom co phieu khong co dong luc moi ve co ban.
